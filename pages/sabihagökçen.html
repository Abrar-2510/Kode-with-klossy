Sabiha Gökçen

Name: Sabiha Gökçen

Date of birth : 22 March, 1913

Date of Death : 22 March, 2001

Education: Üsküdar American Highschool, Turkish War Academy

Her story:

Sabiha Gökçen, the daughter of Mustafa Izzet Bey and Hayriye Hanım. During (turkish founding father) Atatürk's visit to Bursa he decided to adopt her and took her to Ankara.
Later on in life she earned her pilots license and was sent to Russia together with seven male students, for an advanced course in glider and powered aircraft piloting.
At Aviation Scohol, she gained succes and Atatürk recommended she became the first military woman pilot in the world.
ın 1938, she was appointed "chief trainer" of the of the Turkish Aeronautical Association. Sabiha Gökçen flew around the world for a period of 28 years. Her book entitled "A Life Along the Path of Atatürk" was published by the Turkish Aeronautical Association to commemorate Atatürk's 100th birthday
