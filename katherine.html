Katherine Johnson

Name : Katherine Johnson

Date of birth : Aug. 26, 1918

Date of Death : Feb. 24, 2020

Education: B.S., Mathematics and French, West Virginia State College, 1937

Achievments : - One of the NASA/NACA “human computers”.
- First woman in the NASA Flight Research Division to receive author credit on a scientific paper.
- Awarded the Presidential Medal of Freedom and the Congressional Gold Medal,among many other awards, including a NASA facility named after her.
Lived to 101, mother of three, with six grandchildren and eleven great-grandchildren.

Her Story : - The stars were always within reach for Katherine Johnson. Using her mathematics skills, she helped NASA send astronauts to the moon and return them safely home. She also overcame racial and gender hurdles that helped make giant leaps for humankind.
